Gamification

"What we learn with pleasure we never forget. Gamification of learning would be a step in that direction for the organization."

PROJECT GOALS Gamifying the Traing ecperience of the trainees.

This project was a proposed solution for the gafimication of training program for trainees at the cognizant academy. This model is designed to induce a competitive self-learning platform for trainees. This solution was inspired by current hacks used by games to keep the users involved until the very end.

Research

This project is still in the ideation phase.Me With a team of 7 people carried out all the Preliminary procedures including gathering enough data to narrow down the feasability of this idea. We tried to cover as many loopholes as we could and came up with a possible framework that could be integrated with the existing Training program. Please wait for further updates.

TEAMS The Workforce

There were four teams to begin with: Learner, SolutionDesigner, Supervisor and Quiz. Each team had access to their branch of code and after every iteration, the teams came together to merge all of their code into one. I was part of the Solution Designer and worked in collaboration with mine the other teams and inside my team. The primary objective of my team was to create interfaces where an administrator can view all the published/rejected/draft courses/LearningPath as per user requirement
